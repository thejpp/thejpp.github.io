This is D Camp, a curated group of interesting people looking for ways to find personal and professional growth through high value discussion in an environment that gives us time and space to reflect, refine and refocus

Find out more below

  • 5 Days away from it all

    We will decamp from the familar and surround ourselves in the unusual, 5 days in the volcanic island of Feuteventura so we can mix the cerebral and the dicursive with the stimulants of natural beauty and physical activity.

  • A shared experience

    We are renting a 14 person Villa so the conversations can spill into the pool or round the fire, so that we can break bread together , so that we can maximise the oppertunity to learn collectively and to return refreshed and enriched

  • Honest and Open

    Each particpant will prepare one 15 minutes, one 30 minute, and one 1 hour session on a topic that they are passionate about. Chatham House rules are binding to encourage open and though-proking dialouge

  • Dedicated Unstructured Time

    Decompression and reflection can't occur without the time and space to do so. Walks up the volcano or along the beach, reading time by the pool or a dip in the atlantic ocean, there will be time to make time for those.

Stargazing guided by a local astronomer to take advatnage of the dark skies of the Canaries and to help ground ourselves.

Dinner with a guided discussion.Taking a leaf out of the "Crypto Curry Club" playbook as we sit down each night to dine, we will absorb and disucss prepared topics from the group

We want stimualting discussion amongst peers that broaden each of us and germinate or reignite your ideas and plans

We've chosen the Canaries for the access to a range of natural beauty, sun, sand, sea and volcanoes, with a climate that encourages conviviality

Only 12 people will be invited, to balance initmacy with diversity within a trusted and trusting circle

Cost target is £500 (plus flights). We're going in september to keep costs affordable for everyone from new grads to c-suite, we recognised that people at all stages of their career will share goals and have wisdom for the group

  • 14/09/22 - 19/09/22

    Off-season rates, but still nice and warm, travlling on non-peak days for cheaper flights

  • 3 Sessions

    No set requirements, but we want to stimulate highq quality discourse, so we ask each particpant to prepare 3 talks, (15m, 30m, 1hr) on anything they are passionate about. The best book you've ever read, your next startup idea, an AMA, whatever.

  • Minimal Structure

    For Days 2-4 : Breakfast 9 | First Session 10-12 | Lunch and Thinking Time: 12-4 | Evening Session 4-6 | Dinner, Drinks & Discussion 7-10

  • Extra curriculars

    Stargazing is booked in, but we'll look at other adventures to have an an opt-in basis. Wanna try surfing? MTB down a volcano? Some of us recharge in solitude, some of us through activity. We'll keep all options open
